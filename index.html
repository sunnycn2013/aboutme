Learning to Coding

Learning to See

Our mind is not a camera.
Seeing is not a passive act.

“We don’t see things as they are, we see them as we are.”

~Anaïs Nin

You need a Coding eye to Coding, and a non-Coder eye to feel what you Coded.


It is a japanese martial arts concept that describes the stages of learning to mastery.

Follow the Rule

The best way to learn to see is copying the masters. That applies to art as well as to any form of Coding.

- Focus on Coding Fundamental.

- Know everything about the tools you’re using

- No time for questioning **why** or **how**, just do what your master says.
